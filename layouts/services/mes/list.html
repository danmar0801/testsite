Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES)

Streamline production with real-time tracking, batch control, and data-driven insights.

What We Do

At ViridianTechs, we design, build, and validate end-to-end MES solutions tailored to your production floor. We start by mapping your existing workflows and defining critical data points—batch recipes, material traceability, in-process checks—and then integrate seamlessly with your ERP, SCADA, and laboratory systems. Our engineers configure secure, audit-ready electronic batch records, automate work-order execution, and deliver real-time dashboards that let you monitor throughput, quality metrics, and equipment performance at a glance. Finally, we perform full IQ/OQ/PQ validation, train your operators, and provide ongoing support so you get maximum uptime and continuous improvement.

Why You Need MES

In today’s regulated, high-speed manufacturing environments, manual tracking and paper records simply can’t keep up. A modern MES gives you instant visibility into every step of production—so you can enforce SOPs consistently, catch quality deviations before they escalate, and reduce cycle times by automating routine tasks. With real-time data, you’ll make faster decisions, slash scrap and rework, and ensure full compliance with FDA, EMA, and ISO standards. In short, MES transforms your shop floor into a dynamic, data-driven operation that boosts productivity, cuts costs, and drives product quality.
